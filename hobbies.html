I have a longstanding passion for animals and nature, which is reflected in the hobbies I actively pursue. Horseback riding, in particular, has taught me discipline, empathy, and the importance of building trust skills that translate well into both personal and professional environments. I am also a dedicated skier, drawn to the precision and mental focus the sport demands, as well as the rejuvenating effect of high-altitude settings. Hiking is another key interest of mine, offering both a physical challenge and a way to reconnect with nature in its purest form. Additionally, photography allows me to capture and express the beauty of the natural world, deepening my appreciation for its details and moments. These outdoor activities not only enhance my physical resilience and mental clarity but also strengthen qualities such as perseverance, adaptability, and mindfulness. Altogether, my hobbies reflect a strong sense of compassion, a proactive attitude, and a deep appreciation for the natural world.
